About AgriAI

Crop diseases can devastate yields, leading to significant financial losses for farmers worldwide. Early detection and timely intervention are crucial for effective disease management.

AgriAI was developed to address this challenge by leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning to create a comprehensive crop disease prediction and management system.

Our mission is to empower farmers with technology that helps them protect their crops, increase yields, and promote sustainable agricultural practices.
